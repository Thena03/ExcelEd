<div class="bg-white border-l-4 border-green-600 p-4 rounded-lg mb-6 shadow-sm">
<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-center mb-4">Filling Operations</h2>
Filling is used to fill ranges with values so that you do not have to type the values in manually. 
</div>

<div class="bg-white border-l-4 border-green-600 p-4 rounded-lg mb-6 shadow-sm">
<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-center mb-4">How to Fill</h2>
To fill a range of cells, first select a cell and then click the fill icon. The fill icon is at the bottom right corner of the cell and has the icon of a small square. When you hover over the icon, it will change to a thin cross.
<br><br>
<p align="center">
  <img src="{{ '/static/howtofill.png' | relative_url }}" alt="How to Fill example"/>
</p>
<br>
 After selecting the fill icon, select the range of cells you want to fill. This will fill the range with the value that was in the selected cell.
 </div>

<div class="bg-white border-l-4 border-green-600 p-4 rounded-lg mb-6 shadow-sm">
<h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-center mb-4">Purpose of Filling</h2>

<strong>Filling can be used for:</strong>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside"> 
<li> Copying : Copying the same value into a range of cells </li>
<li> Sequences : A sequence is an order. The filling function is used to continue an order of values that has been set.</li>

<li> Dates : to fill in dates in cells
</li>
<li> Functions: You will learn more in a later section.  </li> <br><br> 
<strong>Tip:</strong> the date format depends on your regional language settings 
</ul>
</div>
